WHY EGGS

Sunny Side

Up Eggs!

Ingredients

Large Eggs

Butter

Salt & Pepper

Directions

Melt butter in a medium nonstick skillet over medium heat.

Crack eggs into skillet.

Reduce heat to low; cook 3 to 4 minutes or until egg whites are completely set and yolks begin to thicken.

Serve immediately.

Season to taste with salt and pepper.
